Motivation

Since the advent of social media, researchers have been interested in understanding how people react to different types of media. The emergence of social networks has transformed the way in which information is spread, and social media platforms have become a central hub for communication and information exchange. Therefore understanding how users interact with media on social networks has become critical, especially for: marketers, influencers, and other stakeholders who want to maximize their reach and impact.

~

One approach to understanding user behavior on social media is through the use of object detection and sentiment analysis. Object detection is a computer vision technique that allows users to identify the objects within their posts, while sentiment analysis uses natural language processing to determine the sentiment of the comments associated with a post. By combining these techniques in this project, users can gain visual and quantifiable data on how their posts have performed.
